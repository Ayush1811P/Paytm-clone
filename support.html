Frequently Asked Questions

Wallet & Add Money
Why is my transaction failing during Add Money?
Add Money transactions may fail due to: (1) Hourly cooldown limits - you can only add money once per hour. (2) Daily limits on adding money (₹50,000 maximum). (3) Bank network connectivity failures. Please wait and try again or use another payment mode.
Are there any charges for transferring money to my wallet?
No, adding money to your PayMoney wallet via UPI, Debit Cards, or Net Banking is completely free. No transactional or service fee is charged.
Recharges & Bill Payments
My recharge was successful but the benefits are not received.
Usually, operators process recharges within a few minutes. If it has been more than 2 hours, please check with your operator support or double-check the mobile number/subscriber ID you recharge. You can download the payment receipt from your transaction list as proof.
How do I fetch my Electricity bill?
Go to the Electricity Bill payment screen, select your State, choose the Electricity Board, and input your Consumer Number. Click "Fetch Bill" to view the outstanding amount and due date.
UPI & Security
What is a UPI ID and how do I create it?
A UPI ID is your virtual payment address (e.g. mobile@paymoney). You can link your bank account under the UPI page and set up a custom 4-digit UPI PIN to process direct peer-to-peer transfers.
